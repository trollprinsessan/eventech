Impact/Week 2026

Impact Week is an annual summit dedicated to defining the critical problems of our time and enabling solutions to them. Curated by Norrsken Foundation together with our friends and partners. It gathers the world's greatest thinkers from within business, academia, entrepreneurship, and policy. Join in, and be the leader you've been waiting for. Be part of the solution.
